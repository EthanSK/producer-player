A desktop app for producers who bounce a lot. Version tracking, mastering workspace, album ordering, and more — one window.

Stop Digging Through Files

"v3_final_FINAL_actualfinal.wav" -- sound familiar? Every bounce lives here, organized by song, latest always on top. No more folder archaeology.

Nail Your Tracklist Flow

Drag tracks around until the album feels right. Every reorder saves instantly -- no export-reimport nonsense, just vibes.

"Fix This at 2:34"

Pin notes to exact moments in a track. Click a note, jump right to the spot. Your future self will thank you.

Hear What Your Fans Will Hear

Spotify normalizes to -14 LUFS. Apple Music doesn't. YouTube does its own thing. Preview exactly how each platform will play your track before you release.

Loudness History Graph

Watch integrated, short-term, and momentary loudness unfold in real time. Spot problem sections at a glance and nail your target LUFS with confidence.

Waveform Display

A scrolling, zoomable waveform with a real-time playback cursor. Scrub to any position instantly and see transients and dynamics at a glance.

Stereo Correlation Meter

Keep your stereo image mono-compatible. The correlation meter warns you the moment your mix drifts into phase trouble -- before it costs you on club systems.

Vectorscope

Visualize your stereo field the way mastering studios do. See width, balance, and phase coherence in a live Lissajous display -- catch issues no meter alone can show.

Reference Level Matching

A/B your master against reference tracks at matched loudness. Remove the "louder is better" bias and hear real tonal differences.

Mid/Side Monitoring

Solo mid or side channels with a click. Hear exactly what your centre vocal is doing versus the stereo width -- essential for surgical mastering decisions.

K-Metering

Bob Katz's K-System built right in. Switch between K-12, K-14, and K-20 scales to ensure your dynamics are right for broadcast, streaming, or cinema.

Crest Factor, Clip Count & DC Offset

One-glance diagnostics. Track peak-to-RMS ratio, count digital overs, and detect DC offset before they become problems on release day.

Saved Reference Tracks

Build a personal library of reference tracks that persist across sessions. Instant A/B comparisons with level-matched playback -- no re-importing every time.

Help Tooltips for Beginners

Hover any meter or control for a plain-English explanation. Learn what LUFS, crest factor, and correlation actually mean -- right inside the app, no Googling required.
